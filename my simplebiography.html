Katambula Muzamiru

Professional Mechanic

Hello! My name is Katambula Muzamiru. I am a Kenyan national and a skilled mechanic by profession. I am married and dedicated to both my family and career.

I began my education at the primary level and later advanced through secondary school. I then pursued a diploma in Automotive Engineering, which helped build a strong technical foundation for my career.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience in vehicle repair and maintenance, I have worked on a wide range of vehicles, from small cars to large commercial trucks. My specialties include engine diagnostics, brake systems, suspension, and general service work.

I am passionate about what I do and always strive to deliver high-quality service to ensure customer satisfaction and road safety.
